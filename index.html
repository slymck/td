Introducing TD Overdraft Relief1

With TD Overdraft Relief1, we offer you even more flexibility—including no fee if you overdraw your checking account balance by $50 or less.

How TD Overdraft Relief helps

If you overdraw the available balance in your checking account by $50 or less, we won't charge you an overdraft fee.

More time to prevent fees with Grace Period2

Our Grace Period2 gives you time to fix an overdraft. Just cover the overdrawn balance plus any additional transactions by 11 PM ET the next business day.

Additional overdraft options for you

You'll find automatic coverage with Standard Overdraft Service3 included with most checking accounts1. You might also choose Savings Overdraft Protection, TD Debit Card AdvanceSM* or overdraft options for TD Small Business customers.

TD Small Business Checking Offer:

Cash back offer valid May 1, 2025 through July 31, 2025 for new eligible TD Business Checking accounts. Offer is not valid for existing or recently closed accounts, or if a previous bonus has already been received.

To qualify for the $500 Cash Back Offer:

  • Deposit a minimum of $2,500 within the first 25 days and maintain at least that balance for 90 additional days.
  • Complete 25 qualifying transactions within the first 3 statement cycles.

The bonus will be credited within 180 days after opening, provided the account is active and not closed or restricted.

Additional Information:

TD Debit Card AdvanceSM allows ATM and debit card transactions to be authorized when funds are insufficient. A $35 fee applies if your account is overdrawn by more than $50, with a maximum of 3 fees per day.
